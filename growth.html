My Growth:

Semester One CAP Quiz

For me this project reflected my growth because through the first semester I definitely struggled with creating different things and to cumulatively come together and use all of the skills that I had struggled with and create such a cool project and quiz was very cool especially with all of the different images/ sprite details. The hardest aspect for me is organizing all of the lines of code because when you have 100+ lines of code it can get pretty tricky. The largest obstacle for this project was definitely the math aspects of the code because it got tricky especially when getting overlying answers on the google sheets, then your data is all off and then when people take the quiz they only ever get the same answer and it's just a large domino effect. Definitely when I paid attention in the sprite and overall app organizing designing levels it just helped me add to the overall design and workability of my app. Looking back I would gather more data to create more accurate results for the users.
